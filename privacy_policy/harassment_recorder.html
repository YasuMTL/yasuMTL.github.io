Privacy Policy

Harassment Recorder collect the following personal information:

  • Your current location

How to use this app:

  • Enter your user name (or email address) and password to log in to your Twitter account on browser.
  • Grant permission for the app to access images or video you wish to join on your tweet.
  • Grant permission for the app to use the camera so that you can attach the captured photo or video on your tweet.

Use of Twitter services is subject to Twitter's Privacy Policy
